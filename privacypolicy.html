Privacy Policy

Last updated on: 26 sep 2025.

At Bamboo Home Care Nursing Services, we respect your privacy and treat your personal information with care. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

1. What We Collect

  • When you use our website or services, we may collect:
  • Basic details – name, phone, email, and address.
  • Health information – only if you choose to share it, to help us provide the right care.
  • Payment information – when you pay online (handled through secure providers).
  • Website usage data – like browser type, device, or IP address (used only to improve our site).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • We use your information to:
  • Provide quality home nursing and care services.
  • Arrange and manage appointments.
  • Process payments securely.
  • Answer your questions and respond to requests.
  • Share updates or service information (only if you agree).

3. Who We Share With

  • We do not sell or misuse your data. We only share it with:
  • Our care team (only the information needed to serve you).
  • Secure third-party partners like payment providers.
  • Government or legal authorities, if required by law.

4. How We Protect Your Data

We use strong security measures to protect your personal information. While no system is 100% perfect, we do everything possible to keep your details safe.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can:
  • Ask us what information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections if something is wrong.
  • Ask us to delete your information (unless we must keep it for legal or healthcare reasons).
  • Opt out of marketing messages at any time.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience. You can disable them in your browser settings if you prefer.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are meant for adults and families. We do not collect information from children under 18 without a parent or guardian’s consent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we’ll post the updated version here with a new effective date.
